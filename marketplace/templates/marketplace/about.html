About ProxiFix

Engineering reliability through every kind of repair task.

ProxiFix is built for people who need a cleaner way to handle repairs, maintenance, diagnostics, and technical work. That includes jobs at home, on vehicles, inside engines, around appliances, and across the everyday fix-it requests that usually live in scattered chats and phone calls.

The platform makes it easier to explain the issue, compare workers, understand trust signals, and keep the repair process moving with less confusion from first request to completed job.
